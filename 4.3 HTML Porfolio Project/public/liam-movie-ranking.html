The best films according to Liam

My top 3 films of all time

The Crow

It just has everything in it: Action, Love, Fire, Fight scenes, The main character even does a great guitar solo at on point.
To top it of it has one of the best soundtracks of all time to boot.

Predator

Such an outstanding performance from one of my favourite actors of all time, Arnold Schwartzenegger.
Some very quotable lines throughout and full of suspense.

Jackass

There is just something about several grown men hurting themselves in really silly ways that never fails to make me laugh and cheer me up when I am down.
